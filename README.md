Oziva Business Performance & Marketing Efficiency Dashboard

This project analyzes the business performance of Oziva using a complete end-to-end data analytics workflow, starting from raw data collection to executive-level insights.

The goal of this project was to identify revenue drivers, marketing inefficiencies, and regional growth opportunities for a fast-growing D2C nutrition brand.

Problem Statement

D2C wellness brands often face challenges such as:

• High marketing spend without clear ROI visibility
• Uneven sales performance across cities and product categories
• Difficulty identifying high-performing vs underperforming markets
• Lack of centralized KPI monitoring for leadership teams

This project addresses these issues by building an interactive analytics dashboard that provides actionable business insights.

End-to-End Analytics Workflow
1️⃣ Data Collection (From Scratch)

The dataset was created from scratch to simulate real business scenarios including:

• Sales transactions
• Product categories and pricing
• Marketing spend data
• Geographic market performance
• Time-based sales trends

2️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation (Excel)

Raw data contained inconsistencies and missing values. Using Excel, I performed:

• Standardization of product categories
• Removal of duplicate entries
• Handling missing values
• Date formatting for time-series analysis
• Creating structured tables for modeling

This step ensured data reliability before analysis.

3️⃣ Data Modeling (Power BI)

A star schema model was designed for efficient analysis:

Fact Table
• Sales transactions

Dimension Tables
• Products
• Geography
• Date
• Market Segmentation

This structure enables fast calculations and scalable analytics.

4️⃣ Advanced DAX Metrics

Key business KPIs were created using DAX measures, including:

• Total Revenue
• Total Profit
• Profit Margin %
• ROI (Return on Ad Spend)
• Month-over-Month Growth
• Revenue Year-to-Date
• Total Units Sold

These metrics help monitor financial performance and marketing efficiency.

5️⃣ Interactive Dashboard Design

The Power BI dashboard enables dynamic business exploration using filters and interactive visuals.

Key Visuals Include:

📈 Revenue Trend Analysis (Monthly Growth)
📊 Revenue vs Profit Margin by Category
🌍 Revenue vs ROI by City (Market Performance)
🏆 Top Products by Revenue & ROI
📉 KPI Monitoring Panel for executive reporting

Interactive filters allow analysis by:

• Market Type
• Performance Level
• Ad Spend Efficiency
• Product Category
• Year

Key Business Insights

🔍 Some Tier-1 cities generate high revenue but lower ROI, indicating possible over-investment in marketing.

📍 Certain Tier-2 cities show strong ROI with moderate spending, revealing hidden expansion opportunities.

📊 Product categories contribute unevenly to profits, suggesting portfolio optimization potential.

📉 Marketing budgets can be optimized by shifting spend toward high-efficiency markets.
